Welcome to FoodPanda Niagara, located in beautiful Niagara region.

Enjoy one of our many dishes served fresh from the kitchen every day, like our Kaju Matar Panner with Naan and Haydrabadi Biryani. No matter what you’re in the mood for, there’s something to enjoy.

Our restaurant is right beside the Falls, as well as the Casino, planning an event or celebration is simple. We have plenty of room to host special occasions and parties of all sizes, right in the hub of our restaurant or in the private dining room.

Taking in all the sights and adventures can really work up an appetite, so there’s always a table for you to enjoy a delicious meal with us. Following that, a good night’s sleep can be found at the Niagara Falls Hilton, The Oakes Hotel, Days Inn or Four Points Sheraton, all within walking distance of our restaurant.
