Welcome to FoodPanda Vaughan, located right in the pedestrian-friendly Village at Vaughan Mills, right across from the Vaughan Mills Shopping Centre. We’re not only close to great shopping but also minutes away from the Highway 400, just off the Bass Pro Mills exit.

Enjoy one of our many dishes for dinner served fresh from the kitchen every day, like our Kaju Matar Panner with Naan and Haydrabadi Biryani. No matter what you’re in the mood for, there’s something to enjoy.

If you’re looking for theme park rides or activities, excitement can be found at Canada’s Wonderland or LegoLand. For nature lovers, we suggest a visit to Boyd Conservation Park. Or relax with a movie at Cineplex Vaughan or play a round of golf at the National Golf Club of Canada.

All those activities can really work up an appetite, so we make sure there’s always a table for you to enjoy a delicious meal with us.

You’re also in luck if you work in the area. This location serves many local businesses and is a perfect place for a quick lunch or to unwind when the workday is done. With so many activities nearby, the restaurant is perfect for families, locals, movie goers, date nights and, of course, special occasions as well.
