Welcome to FoodPanda Scarborough, located in the Scarborough City Centre, conveniently close to the Scarborough Town Centre Shopping Mall for nearby shoppers. For those coming right to the Town Centre for a meal, we’re just off the 401 between Brimley and McCowan.

Enjoy one of our many dishes for dinner served fresh from the kitchen every day, like our Kaju Matar Panner with Naan and Haydrabadi Biryani. No matter what you’re in the mood for, there’s something to enjoy.

Just steps away from the restaurant, you can get some shopping done at the Scarborough Town Centre, or catch a movie at the Cineplex Cinemas Scarborough. If you’re on a family outing, why not take the kids to visit the Toronto Zoo?

The Scarborough Civic Centre and University of Toronto Scarborough Campus are also in the area. All those activities can really work up an appetite, so we’ll make sure there’s always a table for you to enjoy a delicious meal with us.

With so many activities nearby, the restaurant is perfect for families, locals, moviegoers, date nights and, of course, special occasions as well.
